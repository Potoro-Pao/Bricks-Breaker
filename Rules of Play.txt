Rules of Play 


1. Press S can start the game

2. Use left and right arrow keys to control the green bat.

3. Press X to stick the ball when the ball come close to the bat.

4. Press (longer) H to start hacker mode to make the ball stronger.

5. Blue Bricks require 1 hit to kill.
.
6. Yellow Bricks require 2 hits to kill. 

7. If all the bricks are cleaned, it's a win.

8. If the ball hit the bottom of the window, it's a loss.

9. Just Enjoy the game.

10. An exe file is downloadable in another branch.
